Find a Roommate, Find a Friend​

Our Mission

The people you surround yourself with have an immense impact on your happiness and well-being. To ensure that college students have the opportunity to thrive, we have created Roommate Finder. Discover friendship, motivation, and prosperity

Features

  • Customization of profile with lifestyle preferences
  • Algorithms to show most compatible users
  • Filters for specific profile settings

Benefits

  • Efficiency of roommate finding process

  • Finding a roommate that you are compatible with

Functions

  • Like or dislike a presented roommate 

  • If two people like each other, they match and can chat in more detail
